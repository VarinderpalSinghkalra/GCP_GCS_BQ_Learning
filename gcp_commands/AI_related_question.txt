Question 1 – BigQuery ML vs Vertex AI

Your analysts want to build a sales forecasting model using SQL without exporting data.

A. Vertex AI Workbench
B. BigQuery ML ✅
C. Dataflow
D. Dataproc

Answer: B

Why?
BigQuery ML lets analysts create and use ML models directly with SQL, avoiding data movement.
